Top US Robotics / VLA / World Model Labs

Leading Robotics Research Labs in the United States

CMU Robotics Institute

Location: Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania

The Robotics Institute is one of the world's most influential robotics research centers focusing on autonomous systems, perception, robot learning, and embodied AI.

Website: https://www.ri.cmu.edu

Stanford Robotics Center

Location: Stanford, California

Stanford Robotics Center coordinates robotics research across Stanford including robot learning, computer vision, manipulation, and autonomous systems.

Website: https://src.stanford.edu

Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research (BAIR)

Location: Berkeley, California

BAIR is a world-leading research lab focusing on robot learning, reinforcement learning, embodied AI, and foundation models.

Website: https://bair.berkeley.edu

MIT CSAIL Robotics

Location: Cambridge, Massachusetts

MIT CSAIL conducts cutting-edge robotics research including robot perception, manipulation, multi-modal models, and autonomous navigation.

Website: https://www.csail.mit.edu

UPenn GRASP Lab

Location: Philadelphia, Pennsylvania

The GRASP Lab is a leading robotics lab working on drone robotics, multi-robot systems, manipulation, and robot perception.

Website: https://www.grasp.upenn.edu
